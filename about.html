About bXo Tutoring

At bXo, we help high school students build strong foundations in biology so they can excel in exams and beyond. Our tutoring approach emphasizes understanding, problem-solving, and confidence — not just memorization.

We value excellence, personalized learning, and a supportive community. Every lesson is tailored to the student’s needs, so they can move forward at the right pace and feel encouraged to ask questions.

Our Story

  • 2015: Started as a small tutoring group for Year 12 students.
  • 2018: Expanded into online tutoring to reach more students.
  • 2022: Launched a structured resource library for HSC Biology modules.
  • Today: Supporting students across Australia with tailored, reliable tutoring.
